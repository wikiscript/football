[[UEFA Euro 2004]] was a [[association football|football]] tournament that took place in Portugal between 12 June and 4 July 2004. The 16 teams that qualified for the competition were required to submit a final 23-man squad by 2 June 2004,<ref>{{cite news |title=Squad deadline looms |url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuro/news/newsid=186121.html |publisher=UEFA.com (Union of European Football Associations) |date=2 June 2004 |accessdate=23 May 2014 }}</ref> although injured squad members could be replaced at any time up to 24 hours before their team's first match.

The players' ages, clubs and caps are accurate as of the start of the tournament's opening day.
