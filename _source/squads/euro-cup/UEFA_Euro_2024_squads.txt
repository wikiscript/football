The age listed for each player is their age as of 14 June 2024, the first day of the tournament. The numbers of caps and goals listed for each player do not include any matches played after the start of the tournament. The club listed is the club for which the player last played a competitive match prior to the tournament. The nationality for each club reflects the national association (not the league) to which the club is affiliated. A flag is included for coaches who are of a different nationality to their team.

==Group A==
===Germany===
Manager: [[Julian Nagelsmann]]

[[Germany national football team|Germany]] started the announcement of their players on 12 May 2024, beginning with the call-up of [[Nico Schlotterbeck]].<ref>{{cite news |date=12 May 2024 |title=Schlotterbeck für EM-Kader nominiert |trans-title=Schlotterbeck nominated for EURO 2024 squad |url=https://www.tagesschau.de/inland/schlotterbeck-nationalelf-100.html |language=de |publisher=[[Tagesschau (Germany)|Tagesschau]] |access-date=14 May 2024}}</ref> On 16 May 2024, 27 players were called up to the preliminary squad.<ref>{{cite news |date=16 May 2024 |title=Nagelsmann gibt vorläufigen Kader für Heim-EM bekannt |trans-title=Nagelsmann announces provisional squad for home European Championship |url=https://www.dfb.de/maenner-nationalmannschaft/news-detail/nagelsmann-gibt-vorlaeufigen-kader-fuer-heim-em-bekannt-261479/ |language=de |publisher=[[German Football Association]] |access-date=30 May 2024}}</ref> On 7 June 2024, the final squad was announced with the exclusion of the goalkeeper [[Alexander Nübel]].<ref>{{cite news |date=7 June 2024 |title=Der EM-Kader steht fest |trans-title=The European Championship squad has been decided |url=https://www.dfb.de/news/detail/der-em-kader-steht-fest-262124/ |language=de |publisher=[[German Football Association]] |access-date=7 June 2024}}</ref>

===Scotland===
Manager: [[Steve Clarke]]

On 22 May 2024, 28 players were called up to [[Scotland national football team|Scotland]]'s preliminary squad.<ref>{{cite news |date=22 May 2024 |title=Steve Clarke names Provisional EURO 2024 Squad |url=https://www.scottishfa.co.uk/news/steve-clarke-names-provisional-euro-2024-squad/ |publisher=[[Scottish Football Association]] |access-date=30 May 2024}}</ref> On 1 June 2024, [[Lyndon Dykes]] withdrew due to injury.<ref>{{cite news |date=1 June 2024 |title=Scotland striker Dykes ruled out of Euro 2024 |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/articles/cqvv496vnpdo |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=1 June 2024}}</ref> On 4 June 2024, [[Ben Doak]] withdrew from the squad and was replaced by [[Tommy Conway]].<ref>{{cite news |date=4 June 2024 |title=Conway called up by Scotland as Doak drops out |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/articles/cglljld4v0vo |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=5 June 2024}}</ref> On 6 June 2024, [[Lewis Morgan (footballer)|Lewis Morgan]] was added to the squad.<ref>{{cite news |date=6 June 2024 |title=Morgan called up to Scotland squad |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/articles/c8vvrpy7q2zo |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=6 June 2024}}</ref> On 7 June 2024, the final squad was announced, with [[Craig Gordon]] and [[John Souttar]] missing out.<ref>{{cite news |date=7 June 2024 |title=Gordon & Souttar cut from Scotland's Euro 2024 squad |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/articles/cjmm73ny1ddo |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=7 June 2024}}</ref>
